Get to know the game

BeTheRef is an in-play App that lets you make calls on key incidents during live matches to see if you know than the ref, pundits, your mates and rival fans.

1/11

Homepage profile

From your Home page you can see your profile information including your name, the team you support and your BeTheRef Score, Average Accuracy and League position.

2/11

Accessing the main menu

You can change your personal information from the Settings tab that's accessible from the main menu.

3/11

Quick access to matches

The next BeTheRef match is displayed on your home page along with a countdown timer. When the match kicks off, tap the words TOUCH HERE TO START to begin playing.

4/11

In play questions

During the match you'll be asked in-play questions about a variety of subjects including real time refereeing decisions, triva, player/team statistics, player ratings, managerial decision and in-play calls.

5/11

In play questions

You have ten seconds to answer each question from a multiple choice selection of answers. The quicker you answer, the more points you'll score... but only if you get it right... obviously.

League tables

After each question your BTR League Position, BTR Score and Decision Accuracy will change to reflect your overall scores and rankings.

8/11

League tables

You can view the League Table from the menu. In case you're wondering, Average Accuracy works in a similar manner to GoalDifference in a normal football league.

9/11

Twitter interaction

If you want to get invloved on Twitter, just use the hashtag #BeTheRefApp. You may even see your comment on the Home Page Twitter feed.
